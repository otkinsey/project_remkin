About Us

HandStack is a web & mobile based platform for community partners to develop and organize their volunteer members, as well as promote events to people nearby. We provide:
  1. A real-time task list. No more crazy-long email threads! All volunteers can easily collaborate with their team and community partners. Anyone can offer to help with a task and mark it off when they're done.
  2. Location-based event publicity. Reach out to your neighbors, not just your Facebook and Twitter friends! Our geolocation publicity tool helps people nearby to find your event.
We help build real movement and impact. Everyone has different talents and resources, and we want to help organizations to mobilize all talents and resources from people involved. We're just a fun bunch of guys and gals with love for technology and community building. Feel free to give us your ideas & feedback about the app!
